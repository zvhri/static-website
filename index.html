Dawson College

Location: 3040 Sherbrooke St W, Montreal, Quebec, H3Z 1A4

About

Dawson College is an English-language CEGEP (Collège d'enseignement général et professionnel) located in the Notre-Dame-de-Grâce neighbourhood of Montreal, Quebec. It is the largest English-language CEGEP in Quebec, serving approximately 10,000 students.

Founded in 1969, Dawson offers both pre-university and career/technical programs leading to the Diplôme d'études collégiales (DEC). The college operates under the Quebec CEGEP system, which sits between secondary school and university.
